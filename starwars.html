AngularThe Fourth Awakens

The web was a dangerous place for developers to visit

DOM coupling was inevitable, watchers watching you from every corner, and no conventions whatsoever.



Then a small team of brave coders (that get paid a ton by Google) started a movement to destroy the evil forces that withheld developers from being productive and happy.



The (core) team decided to build a platform so great and wonderful, that all other frameworks will bow down and surrender to the mighty emperor of the web.




But that was too hard so they built Angular.




Nonetheless, web developers from the entire galaxy started using Angular, and it has become extremely popular, and was able to defeat evil competitors like Backbone and Ember.



After a long period of peace in the kingdom, a new contender have arose, and is threathing the throne.

This is of course.....
